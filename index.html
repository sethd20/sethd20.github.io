San Antonio Spurs

The Spurs are a basketball team located in San Antonio, Texas and is currently the "model organization" in the NBA.Currently they are the most successful team in the NBA with the best winning percentage, 18 straight playoff appearances, and five championships within that span.

The success of this team is due to Gregg Popovich, the coach, and Tim Duncan. But other players have contributed to the team such as Tony Parker and Manu Ginobili. These three players form the "Big 3." Now with an up and coming star in Kawhi Leonard, the Spurs have many more postseason appearances to come.

New England Patriots

The Patriots are a football team located in Foxborough, Massachusetts. The Patriots are currently one of the more successful teams with 11 playoff appearances in the past 13 years along with four Superbowl wins within that time.

The team's success is mainly due to player and coach tandem of Bill Belichick and Tom Brady, the quarterback. Many players have contributed to the Patriots' success such as receivers, Randy Moss and Wes Welker, tight end Rob Gronkowski, and defensive tackle. ALthough many of these players have come and gone, as long as Tom Brady continues to play well, and Bill Belichick remains coach, the Patriots will continue to be a contender for the the Superbowl.
